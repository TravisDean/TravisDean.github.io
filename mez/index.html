MEZ - An Augmented Reality based Music Visualizer

MEZ combines the power of realtime music visualization with the AR possibilities of upcoming devices like Microsoft's Project Hololens. Right now it can be used in normal virtual modes on your desktop or as an app for Android.

Data is taken from both raw frequency analysis and higher level musical information when possible. This was implemented early on through a MIDI interface for single notes, and ways of effectively visualizing structures corresponding to concepts like intervals and scales are being considered.
